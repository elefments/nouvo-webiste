**FAQ 01:**
H5: Which advertising platform is better Google or Meta?
H4: It depends on the goal. Google Ads performs best for intent-based searches the user is already looking for what you offer. Meta Ads performs best for brand awareness, remarketing and audiences that aren't searching yet. Often the two work best together.

**FAQ 02:**
H5: How much budget do paid ads require?
H4: It depends on the industry, competition and goal. Local businesses can start effectively with €300-€500 monthly ad spend. We provide an estimate based on your market before any commitment.

**FAQ 03:**
H5: What is performance marketing?
H4: Performance marketing is paid digital marketing measured exclusively by results: ROAS, CPA, conversion rate. Every euro is tracked and optimized. There's no "impressions as success" only measurable outcomes.

**FAQ 04:**
H5: Does TikTok Ads work for professional businesses?
H4: Yes if your audience is there. With a strong presence in Greece and CPM lower than Meta, it's effective for e-commerce, hospitality and B2C brands that want to build awareness quickly.

**FAQ 05:**
H5: Do I need a content strategy if I'm already running paid ads?
H4: Yes. Content is fuel for ads without quality content, paid campaigns underperform. Additionally, content strategy builds organic traffic and brand authority that continue to deliver value even when advertising stops.

**FAQ 06:**
H5: How do you measure campaign performance?
H4: With specific KPIs agreed before launch: ROAS, CPA, CTR, conversion rate, cost per lead. We provide monthly reporting with results analysis and strategic adjustments no vanity metrics.

**FAQ 07:**
H5: Why don't you start marketing without assessing the website?
H4: Because the traffic ads bring lands on your pages. If those pages don't convert, the ad spend is wasted. We check the infrastructure first then we start performance marketing.
