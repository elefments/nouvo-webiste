### FAQ SECTION

**Section Eyebrow (Label):**
FAQ

**H2 (`display-2`):**
Questions worth answering.

**FAQ 01:**
H5: What should a corporate website include?
H4: Clear company and service presentation, credibility proof (portfolio, testimonials), easy access to contact information and CTAs leading to specific actions. Every page serves a purpose in the conversion funnel.

**FAQ 02:**
H5: How long does development take?
H4: Typically 3 to 6 weeks. Depends on the number of pages, design complexity and how quickly content is provided by the company.

**FAQ 03:**
H5: Why not use a ready-made template?
H4: Templates constrain architecture, create technical debt and slow the site down with unnecessary code. A custom corporate website is designed exactly for the company's needs without compromise.

**FAQ 04:**
H5: Does my company legally need a corporate website?
H4: Yes, if you're an IKE. Under N.4072/2012, every IKE is required to have a corporate website within one month of incorporation. Beyond the legal requirement, it's the primary credibility and client acquisition tool.

**FAQ 05:**
H5: Which CMS do you use?
H4: WordPress the most flexible platform with an extensive ecosystem. We deliver with full training so the company can manage content independently.

**FAQ 06:**
H5: Is the website SEO-ready?
H4: Yes. Optimized meta tags, Organization schema, canonical tags, Core Web Vitals compliance and clean code SEO is built in from the start, not added afterwards.

**FAQ 07:**
H5: Can I have a bilingual corporate website?
H4: Yes. We build multilingual websites with correct hreflang implementation, separate URLs per language and an SEO strategy for each target market.
