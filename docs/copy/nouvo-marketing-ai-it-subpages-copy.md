FAQ:
H5: Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace: which to choose?
H4: Depends on workflows and team preferences. If the business already uses Windows and Office applications, Microsoft 365 is the natural choice. If it primarily uses web-based tools, Google Workspace is more flexible. We assess and recommend.

H5: What are SPF, DKIM and DMARC?
H4: Technical DNS settings that prevent others from sending email on behalf of your domain and improve deliverability. Without them, your emails may end up in spam.

H5: Can you transfer old emails to the new system?
H4: Yes. We handle full email migration with complete history retention. Zero data loss.

H5: What happens if my domain expires?
H4: We monitor expiry dates and notify you in advance. We also handle automatic renewal if preferred.
